%name Help

This document will help you use %name properly.
Please read all the way through.


Tabs

To open a new tab, click on the "Browser" menu, and click "New tab", or hit CTRL+T. To close a tab, middle click it, click "Close tab" in the "Browser" menu, or hit CTRL+W


Windows

To open a new window, click on the "Browser" menu and click "New window". To close a window, simply close it like you would normally. When all windows have been closed, %name terminates itself, so do not close all windows when downloading a file.


Downloading Files

To download a file, enter the URL for it, or click on a link pointing to it. A dialog will open asking if you want to open it or save it. If the file is an image, you can open it with the built-in image viewer. If the file is media such as audio or video, you may open the file in VLC Media Player, if installed.
To set the default download location, click on the "Edit" menu and click "Set download location" and enter the path you would like to have as your downloads location.


Homepage

When you start %name, your homepage will be displayed.
Your current homepage is %homepage.
To set a new homepage, click on the "Edit" menu, and click "Set homepage". Enter a valid URL for your homepage, and it will be set.


Playing YouTube Videos

To play a YouTube video, simply visit the video's address. Once the page has loaded, you wil be asked if you want to play the video in VLC. Click "OK" to play the video.


Bookmarks

To add a bookmark, click on the "Bookmarks" menu and click on "Add bookmarks", or hit CTRL+D. To view, edit, and delete bookmarks, click on the "Bookmarks" menu and click "View bookmarks".


Search Engines

To search for a term, type it in the address bar and press the "Go" button or hit enter. To set your search engine, click the "Edit" menu and click "Set search URL".
